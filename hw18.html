User Research

I conducted a minimum of 5 Interviews and created online surveys. I had to come up with research plans and a series of questions so that I could interview my users. User Interface Design

After gathering data from my User research, I was able to go into the ideation process of design and use several techniques like an Affinity diagram, and empathy map and create an ideal user persona. Where I was able to find the common problem and therefore come up with a solution.
